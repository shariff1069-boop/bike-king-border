Bike Trails

Incredible trails right on our doorstep

Explore the Borders

The Scottish Borders is home to some of the best mountain biking in the UK. From gentle forest paths to adrenaline-fuelled descents, there is something for every type of rider. Our shop is perfectly located near several world-class trail centres including Glentress and Innerleithen.

Trail Video

🟢 Beginners Trails

Perfect for families and first-time mountain bikers. Smooth surfaces, gentle gradients and easy navigation.

Glentress Green Trail

Distance: 5km

Difficulty: Easy

A flat, scenic forest loop ideal for beginners and younger riders.

Tweed Valley Family Route

Distance: 8km

Difficulty: Easy

A gentle riverside route with beautiful views over the Tweed Valley.

🔵 Intermediate Trails

Some climbing involved with more technical sections. Suitable for riders with a bit of experience.

Glentress Blue Trail

Distance: 12km

Difficulty: Intermediate

Fast flowing singletrack with some short sharp climbs and brilliant descents.

Innerleithen Blue Run

Distance: 10km

Difficulty: Intermediate

A varied route mixing forest tracks with open hillside riding.

🔴 Extreme Trails

For experienced riders only. Steep, technical and fast. Full suspension bike and protective gear strongly recommended.

Innerleithen DH Red/Black

Distance: 6km

Difficulty: Extreme

One of the best downhill trails in Scotland. Fast, technical and seriously exciting.

Glentress Black Trail

Distance: 18km

Difficulty: Extreme

The full Glentress black loop – a serious challenge with stunning Borders scenery.
