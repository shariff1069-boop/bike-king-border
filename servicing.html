Why Choose Us?

Bike King Borders is the only dedicated bike repair and servicing centre in the Borders region. Our qualified mechanics have years of experience working on all types of bikes. We guarantee all our work and use only quality parts.

You'll always receive a quote before any work begins, and we'll never carry out unnecessary repairs.
